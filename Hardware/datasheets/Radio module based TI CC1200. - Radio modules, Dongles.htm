Details

Radio module based TI CC1200.

N511CS CC1200 module designed for 169M, 433M, 868M, 915MHz band. Up to 15dBm output power and Sensitivity down to -123dBm.

CC1200 module designed by 4-layer board. IoT, intelligent home, wireless meter reading, scientific research and medical treatment, and medium and long-distance wireless communication equipment. Up to 15dBm output power and Sensitivity down to -123dBm. CC1200 module leads all pins out for secondary development.

N511CS is suitable for free frequency band in EU and USA.
